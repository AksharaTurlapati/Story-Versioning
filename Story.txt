Once upon a time in a forest there lived a Monkey who resided on a berry tree. This was on the bank of a river. In the same river, lived a Crocodile and his wife. One day the Crocodile cam to the banks of the river and rested under the tree. The kind hearted Monkey saw him and offered some berries. The Crocodile came back the next day for more, as he loved them. Days passed by and the two creatures became really good friends.
